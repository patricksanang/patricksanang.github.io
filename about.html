Goals

1 - Customer Satisfaction

Two customers have to be satisfied with my work:

  • The client who asked the work to be done. "Communication, Collaboration and Deadline respect" are my motto in work. At the end, the SLA (Service Level Agreement) needs to be aligned with the client.
  • The end user. In fact, customer experience through workflow processes implemented is a main concern for me.
In order to achieve that, I love to use SCRUM as my agile method of software development.

2 - Optimization in using of resources

Since the resources used (deployment costs, memory, server resources, end user device capacities, etc...) are not unlimited, they are always a concern for me to be optimized.

3 - Well Maintained Sites

Through all the form of maintenance: preventive, corrective, adaptative, perfective.
